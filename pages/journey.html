My journey so far has been a rollercoaster of experiences, challenges, and growth. I have had the opportunity to work with some amazing people, learn from the best, and grow as a professional. Here is a brief overview of my journey so far.

My career in logistics and sales (2006 - 2020)

Logistics Employee | De Mandemakers Groep (2006 - 2016)

My career started in logistics at De Mandemakers Groep, where I was responsible for loading trucks, checking products and preparing orders for shipment. In this role, I gained broad experience with logistics processes, such as order processing, transport planning, goods management and export/expedition. During ten years, I learned the ins and outs of the sector and developed a strong sense of accuracy and efficiency. In order to deepen my logistics knowledge, I followed the MBO2 Logistics Employee training (2010 - 2011) via ROC Midden Nederland during this period. Here I learned the basic principles of logistics processes such as order picking, inventory and stock management. Because I wanted more responsibility, I subsequently followed the MBO3 Logistics Team Leader training (2011 - 2012) via ROC Midden Nederland. This training gave me insight into team management, warehouse layout and administrative processes.


Logistics Employee | FPE Seals (2016 - 2018)

After my time at DMG, I made the switch to FPE Seals, where I could further develop my logistics skills. Here the focus was on accurately measuring products in different units, processing orders and providing customer service at the physical counter. My technical insight and eye for detail came in handy in the hydraulic sector, where precision is of great importance.


Logistics Teamlead | FPE Seals (2018 - 2020)

Shortly afterwards, I was given the opportunity to progress to the position of Logistics Teamlead within FPE Seals. In this role, I was responsible for coordinating the logistics process, including order picking, processing and shipping. I also helped with the reorganization of the warehouse to improve efficiency. This was a valuable experience in which I not only sharpened my leadership skills, but also worked with tools such as Adobe and Excel for inventory management and reporting.




Sales Trainee | FPE Seals (Aug 2020 - Oct 2020)

Looking for new challenges, I made the switch to the sales department as a Sales Trainee. Here the focus was on customer contact, order processing and product service. I had the opportunity to support both regular and new customers and process their requests efficiently. I also learned to recognize and advise on catalog products based on customer needs. This role gave me valuable commercial experience and strengthened my communication skills.




Switch to IT (2020 - present)

Transition to IT | Retraining program (Oct. 2020 - Apr. 2022)

My interest in IT started at a young age through my passion for gaming, discovering software and tinkering with hardware. In 2020, I decided to give my career a new direction and focus entirely on IT. During this period, I immersed myself in various programming languages ​​and technologies, including:

  • Programming languages: .NET, Python, Node.JS, Java, C#, C++, TypeScript
  • Frameworks and technologies: ASP.NET MVC, Django, Angular, React.JS
  • Databases: MySQL
  • Concepts: Object Oriented Programming (OOP)

During my transition to the IT sector, I took the "Programming for Beginners" course through Winc Academy on my own initiative, which gave me a solid foundation in software development. To strengthen my foundation in IT, I followed a retraining program for the position of Service Desk employee via the UWV. In addition, I followed various online courses and self-studies to further develop my technical skills.






IT Helpdesk Employee | Sligro Group BV (Apr 2022 - Oct 2022)

After my retraining, I got my first IT job as an IT Helpdesk Employee at Sligro Group BV via Acknowledge. In this role, I supported end users with IT issues and worked with various systems and tools, including:

  • TopDesk
  • Office 365 & Azure
  • Commandline & AS400
  • Hardware, laptops, phones & telephony

During this secondment, my service skills were tested to the limit, and I had the opportunity to support users with a variety of IT challenges. This strengthened my problem-solving skills and technical expertise.

Certificates and courses obtained during this period:

  • ICT Support Helpdesk MBO-4 (IT-University B.V., Dec. 2021 - Feb. 2022)
  • Microsoft 365 Fundamentals (MS-900)
  • ITIL v4 Foundation(not certified, but coursed eventually through recruitment company)


SupportDesk Developer/Lead Helpdesk | Comaxx.nl (Oct. 2022 - Oct. 2023)

After my experience at the helpdesk, I grew into a more technical role as SupportDesk Developer/Lead Helpdesk at Comaxx.nl. Here I was responsible for investigating and solving IT issues at customers, working with various systems and platforms such as:

  • Programming languages ​​& frameworks: PHP, Laravel
  • Content Management Systems:WordPress, Magento
  • Containerization & version control:Docker, GitLab, GitBash, PowerShell
  • Software & tools: PHPStorm, Jira, ZenDesk, CMD

In addition to solving IT problems, I supported development projects and worked with management to ensure an efficient support structure.


ICT Specialist | Cloetta BV (Feb. 2024 - March 2025)

Via Linden-IT, I was appointed as ICT Operations & Security Specialist at Cloetta BV in 2024, where my focus is on helpdesk and security-related tasks. I support end users with software, hardware and remote solutions, and also work on security projects with teams such as hosting, management and board & directors.

Systems and technologies I work with here:
  • TopDesk, SCCM & Active Directory
  • Command Line & Cisco
  • Remote Desktop & Microsoft 365
  • MS Copilot (AI implementation for various departments)

Due to the international environment and technical challenges at Cloetta BV, I have further developed my skills in cloud solutions, hybrid working, administrator and management roles.


Education and technical training (2024 - present)

In addition to my work, I continue to actively delve into IT areas such as security, infrastructure and networks. I started with the HBO Module Design and Programming (Hogeschool NCOI, 2024) and am working on various certifications and courses from free online platforms such as:

  • Udemy
  • Codecademy
  • Coursera
  • LinkedIn Learning

In addition, I experiment with Linux Ubuntu and Kali Linux to further expand my knowledge of networks, security and server management. I am also currently working on developing my own website as a digital CV, further strengthening my web development skills and online presence.


ServiceDesk Specialist | DLL (April 2025 - Present)

Present day, I work as a IT Service Desk Officer at DLL(De Lage Landen) via Linden IT, where I support end users with IT issues and work on various projects. Also in my free time, I continue to develop my technical skills and knowledge in the field of IT, and I am working on obtaining various certifications and courses. From this point on I will also gain more experience from the knowledge I have gained so far and continue to grow as an IT professional.
